ConnectAI leverages reinforcement learning and natural language processing (NLP) to facilitate meaningful connections based on the MBTI personality types. The MBTI categorizes individuals into 16 personality types, such as ISTJ, INFP, and ESTP, determined by preferences for Introversion (I) or Extraversion (E), Sensing (S) or Intuition (N), Thinking (T) or Feeling (F), and Judging (J) or Perceiving (P). Each type embodies distinct traits—ISTJs are practical and reliable, INFPs are idealistic and empathetic, ESTPs are energetic and adaptable—offering insights into how individuals perceive, decide, and relate. This approach enhances understanding and fosters effective communication across diverse personalities, empowering ConnectAI to forge connections that are both compatible and enriching.
